// 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Davis — a network engineer and cybersecurity specialist based in Virginia Beach. My day job involves working on Navy shipboard networks and enterprise WiFi deployments, and my nights usually involve whatever project has my attention that week.

I've got a deep interest in RF and radio engineering — from HAM radio and SDR work to designing my own VHF hardware from scratch. There's something satisfying about getting a signal where it shouldn't be able to go.

Outside of the technical side, I'm usually hunting, shooting, or out in the field. Eagle Scout background means I still think in terms of systems and preparation — habits that translate directly into how I approach infrastructure problems.
